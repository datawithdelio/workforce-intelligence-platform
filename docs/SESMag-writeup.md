# SESMag Writeup

Workforce Intelligence Platform is a strong fit for SESMag personas because it prioritizes clarity, structure, and low-friction decision making across the whole employee workflow. The platform gives employees a simple way to review and request profile updates, gives managers a clear approval queue with side-by-side comparisons, and gives admins a readable dashboard for KPIs, notifications, and explainable risk signals. Instead of hiding important actions in dense menus or technical language, the product keeps the next step obvious on every page.

The design is especially supportive for DAV because it reduces ambiguity and cognitive load. Every form uses visible labels, statuses are shown with both text and icon instead of color alone, buttons are large enough to tap comfortably, and wording stays plain and direct, such as “Waiting for manager review” instead of internal system terms. Private versus public information is explained clearly, workflows are broken into understandable steps, and the dashboard surfaces only the most important metrics first. These choices make the system easier to scan, less stressful to navigate, and more usable for people with different comfort levels around technology.

The project also supports a broad range of users by combining strong visual hierarchy with responsive layouts and audit-friendly records. Employees can understand what they changed and what happens next, managers can review updates quickly without losing context, and admins can interpret analytics without needing to read raw database output. In that way, the app is not just technically complete, but intentionally designed to serve multiple SESMag personas while giving special attention to DAV-style usability needs.
